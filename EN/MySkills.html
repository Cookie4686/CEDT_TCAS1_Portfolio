Lifelong Learning

Lifelong learning is an important skill to have in my life, which makes me continuously learn lots of concepts in a variety of fields. In this section I will talk about some of those which included computing and academic fields.

Grade 10 (Age: 16)
In the first semester of grade 10 I have an online class (because of COVID-19 pandemic) which has a ton of stuff that distracts me from focusing on studying, which in turn makes my score drop. But now in the present I have learned from my past and I can classify what is distracting me from working, so I have more motivation and determination now than in the past and I also believe that I could do better in an online class than before.

Grade 11
In grade 11 things got chaotic for me because I had to work in the student council and also participate in various activities that my senior/junior invited me to. In July-August 2022 I enrolled in RSU Rookie Game Dev 2022 online course about game development with Unity. In the same month I also participated in a science competition on the topic of “Green Technology and Innovation for Health and Environment” in which I got second place. In October I enrolled in the Gamer to Coder 2022 online course about web development, which to be fair I’m quite lazy and didn’t get much out of it. Finally in December I participated in the Bangmod Hackathon 2023 competition in which I learned more about data structure and algorithms in C.

Grade 12
In the final year of high school I focus on my future studying in the university. I learned some crucial skills that I need mainly on financial and investing in the first semester. Other than that I focus on making my website portfolio, learning about engineering and computing related-fields. During my holiday in September-October I learned about Chula MOOC and Cisco Skills for All, which help me improve my skills and knowledge greatly.

As you can see in my past, Lifelong Learning is an important factor in my life that makes me continuously improve myself. And Now I have more Motivation and Self-Awareness just by learning because I know what I like or dislike from learning various topics.
